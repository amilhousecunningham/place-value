What is Place Value? Place value is how we understand the value of each digit in a number. The position of a digit tells us its value. For example, in 735, the '7' is in the hundreds place (meaning 7 hundreds), the '3' is in the tens place (meaning 3 tens), and the '5' is in the ones place (meaning 5 ones). Let's discuss more! Imagine numbers are like rooms, and each number has its own place in the house:

  • Hundreds: The first room is where the largest digits have their space.
  • Tens: The second room is where the middle-sized digits live.
  • Ones: The last room is where the smallest digits live.
